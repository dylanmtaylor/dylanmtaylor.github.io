Clippy High Resolution Application Icon Done

20 Nov 2010

Because of the professional nature of Clippy, my clipboard</a> management application, in comparison to some of my other applications, I decided to go with a really simple, yet elegant and polished look on this one. Because I recreated the original Clippy icon as a scalable vector image</a>, creating this new icon was a piece of cake, and there's literally no loss of quality at all. Most of the work was playing with the drop shadow</a> behind the clipboard until it looked just right. This new icon is already up on the Android Market as I'm writing this post.
